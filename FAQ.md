## Gameplay

Q: How to use parry?
A: Press Y+LT (controller) or E (keyboard). Swaps to Hand of Malenia and parries.

Q: What happens on parry?
A: Orange glow, sound, particles, stack counter increases, buffs applied for 15 seconds.

Q: Stacks go infinite?
A: No. Capped at 10 stacks. Resets if 15 seconds pass without parry.

Q: How long do buffs last?
A: 15 seconds. BUT resets every time you parry. Keep parrying to keep buffs.

Q: What buffs?
A: At 10 stacks: +3% damage, +5% stamina, +2% stance break.

Q: Works on all enemies?
A: Yes! Bosses, humanoids, everything parryable.

Q: PvP?
A: Yes! Works in PvP. Opponent sees glow and parry.

Q: Multiplayer?
A: Yes! Seamless Co-op fully supported. Your buffs only affect you.

## Customization

Q: Change keybinds?
A: Edit keybind_config.json. Change "Y", "LT" to other buttons. Restart game.

Q: What buttons available?
A: Controller: A, B, X, Y, LB, LT, RB, RT, Start, Back
Keyboard: A-Z, 0-9, F1-F12, Space, arrows, etc.

Q: Custom sounds?
A: Yes. Add .wem files to sounds/ folder. Or disable in config.json.

Q: Disable particles?
A: Edit config.json: `"particle_effects": { "enabled": false }`

Q: Disable glow?
A: Edit config.json: `"orange_glow": { "enabled": false }`

Q: Disable counter?
A: Edit config.json: `"ui_counter": { "enabled": false }`

Q: Volume?
A: Edit config.json: `"volume": 0.5` (0.0 to 1.0)

## Multiplayer

Q: Partner need mod?
A: No! Only you need it. Partner just sees effects.

Q: I disable, partner can still use?
A: Yes! Type "toggle" to disable yours. Theirs still works.

Q: My buffs affect partner?
A: No. Your buffs local only. Partner unaffected.

Q: Lag?
A: No. Very optimized. No lag in co-op.
